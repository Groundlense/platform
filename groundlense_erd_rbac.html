Two-Level RBAC: (1) Company-level roles — what a user can do within their own company account. (2) Project-level roles — what a company (and its users) can do on a specific project. Both levels combine to determine final access.

Key Principle — Billing ≠ Permissions: Who pays (billing_company_id on project) has NO effect on data access rights. A company's project_role determines what they can see and do. Payment only determines whether boring data is "unlocked" for download — not whether they can view live data.
Data Ownership Rule: The geotech agency that captured the boring data OWNS it. IE firm can review and annotate but cannot delete original records. Any edit creates a new revision in audit_log with IS code justification. Original record is never overwritten.
